BREEZE

Explore ancient ruins and seaside caves in this tropical paradise. Wide open spaces and long sightlines make range combat crucial, so stay alert and gear up before you relax in the Breeze.

LOTUS

Step into Lotus, a mystical map with three spike sites and unique features like rotating doors and a breakable wall. With endless tactical options, players are encouraged to explore every corner.

SUNSET

Inspired by the streets of Los Angeles, Sunset brings a classic two-site layout with three key lanes. Mechanical doors and tight angles create fast, dynamic fights across the cityscape.

ICEBOX

Fight in the frozen depths of the Kingdom’s excavation site. Snow-covered factories and ziplines create fast-paced vertical battles where movement and map control are key to victory.

HAVEN

Haven breaks tradition with three spike sites, pushing players to adapt and strategize. Its diverse layouts and fast rotations reward creativity and sharp tactical thinking every round.
